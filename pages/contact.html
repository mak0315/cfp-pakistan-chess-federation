Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a FIDE ID / rating? +

To obtain a FIDE ID, you must participate in a FIDE-rated tournament organized by CFP or an affiliated provincial association. Contact us via email at amer.chess@gmail.com to inquire about the next rated event near you. CFP's Rating Officer Muhammad Waqar handles all FIDE registration submissions.

How do I become a CFP member? +

Membership is open to all chess players in Pakistan. Contact your provincial chess association or CFP directly to register. Visit our Membership page for details.

How do I organize a FIDE-rated tournament? +

To organize a FIDE-rated event, you need to apply through CFP. We submit the tournament for FIDE approval. Contact us with your proposed tournament details — dates, location, format, and prize fund. Our technical committee will guide you through the process.

How are Pakistan's FIDE ratings updated? +

FIDE updates ratings monthly. After each FIDE-rated tournament, CFP submits results to FIDE. Ratings are then updated on the 1st of each month. You can check your current rating at ratings.fide.com.

How can I represent Pakistan internationally? +

Selection for international events (Chess Olympiad, Asian Championships, etc.) is based on FIDE rating and performance in national tournaments. The National Chess Championship is the primary qualification route. Contact CFP to express your interest in international selection.
