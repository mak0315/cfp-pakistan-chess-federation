Process

How to Join

Getting your CFP membership is simple. Follow these steps to join Pakistan's chess family.

1

Contact CFP

Email amer.chess@gmail.com or message us on Facebook/WhatsApp expressing your interest.

2

Submit Details

Provide your full name, CNIC/B-Form number, date of birth, province, and contact information.

3

Pay Membership Fee

Pay the annual membership fee via bank transfer or at a CFP event. Junior membership is free.

4

Get Your FIDE ID

Participate in your first CFP-rated tournament. CFP submits your results to FIDE and obtains your official FIDE ID.

FIDE

Getting Your FIDE ID

A FIDE ID is your international chess identity. Once rated, you can play in FIDE tournaments worldwide, track your rating at ratings.fide.com, and work toward FIDE titles — Candidate Master (CM), FIDE Master (FM), International Master (IM), or Grandmaster (GM).
